About OpenServe

We believe in free and open advertising for everyone.

Our Mission

OpenServe exists to democratize online advertising. We believe every website publisher deserves a chance to monetize their content without giving up most of their revenue to tech giants.

Who We Are

We're a team of developers and marketers who have worked in online advertising for over 15 years. We've seen how traditional ad networks take 40-50% of publisher revenue. We decided to do something about it.

What We Offer

A complete self-hosted ad platform that puts you in control. No middleman, no fees, no contracts. Just you and your revenue.
